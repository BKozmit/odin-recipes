Reuban

Description

A delicious melt that many find themselves ordering in a diner! This dish balances the fatty taste with the corned beef with saurcraut as well as the rye bread!
A good dish for a quick yet delightful lunch!


Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 8 slices rye bread
  • 8 slices deli sliced corned beef
  • 8 slices Swiss cheese
  • 1 cup sauerkraut, drained
  • 1/2 cup Thousand Island dressing

Directions

  1. Preheat a large skillet or griddle on medium heat.
  2. Lightly butter one side of bread slices. Spread non-buttered sides with Thousand Island dressing.
  3. On 4 bread slices, layer 1 slice Swiss chees, 2 slices corned beef, 1/4 cup sauerkraut and second slice of swiss cheese. Top with remaining bread slices, buttered sides out.
  4. Grill sandwiches until both sides are golden brown, about 5 minutes per side. Serve Hot.
